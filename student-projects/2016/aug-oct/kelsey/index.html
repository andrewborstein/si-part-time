About

Tiny Empire is an affordable housing option that gives students the opportunity to own their own home in four years. The units are environmentally and economically sustainable.

All custom built homes are tailor made to meet your individual needs. Residents can choose from three different floor plans, and from add-ons such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and either composting or incinerating toilets.

Residents have unlimited access to the fitness center, study lounge, and community garden. The community dog park gives pets a chance to get off the leash and play!
