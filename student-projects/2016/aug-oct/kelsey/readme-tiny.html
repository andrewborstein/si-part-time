About Tiny Empire is an affordable housing option that gives students the opportunity to own their own home in four years. They are environmentally and economically sustainable. Custom built. Mobile, 1bed 1 bath, rainwater harvesting, solar panels, composting or incinerating toilets, or plugged in camp ground. RV certified and road legal. Community will offer amenities- fitness center, study lounge, as well as community garden and dog park. Design your own tiny home to meet your needs and desires. Choose from 3 different floor plans, customizable, rooftop space/balcony, porches, toilets, affordability(no features added). breakfast nook. At the end of the four year lease, you can keep the home, or sell it back to Tiny Empire for 10,000. 8 man production team, 2 weeks to build. 400 sqft. with loft handicap version of the home, no loft Built for one.
